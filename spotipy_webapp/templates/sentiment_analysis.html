To see the effect of various components (lyrics and song attributes) on song genre classification, I built two models to classify a song. Given the list of genres Spotify tags this song with, this information provides an interesting glance into how the song reads and feels. Many genres today are a combination of two larger genres and my hope is that this heuristic model gives you a feel for what "pop rap" really means in terms of characteristics and content.
